MAX20480A

Pin Description

PIN NAME FUNCTION
1 IN1 Input voltage monitor 1.
2 IN2 Input voltage monitor 2.
3 IN3 Input voltage monitor 3.
4 IN4 Input voltage monitor 4.
5 EN0 Enable input 0. Raise/lower the EN0 input to indicate a transiton from OFF -> ON/ON -> OFF, respectively in the system
6 GND Ground. connect all grounds together at the EP
7 EP1 Enable input 1. Rasie/lower the EN1 input to indicate a transtion from SLEEP -> ON/ON -> SLEEP, respectively in the system
8 VDD Input supply voltage connect a 0.1uF capacitor between VDD and GND and place close to the IC For excessive VDD transients with edge rates >40mV/us an RC Filter is reduired in the VDD supply
9 GND Ground. conect all grounds together at the EP.
10 GND Ground. conect all grounds together at the EP.
11 GND Ground. conect all grounds together at the EP.
12 GND Ground. conect all grounds together at the EP.
13 RESET RESET output. Open-drain output that signals a status change. Can be mapped to any combination of input monitors to indicate they are within nominal operating range.

Connect to logic supply with a pullup resistor.
14 ADDR I2C Address select. connect to GND or VDD, with or without a 100KOhm pullup resistor, to set the I2C address.
15 SDA I2C data I/O.
16 SCL I2C clock input.
- EP Exposed pad. Connect to ground. Dose not serve as a substitute for a proper GND pin connect connection

Absolute Maximum Ratings

VDD to GND -0.3V to +6V
EN0, EN1 to GND -0.3V to +6V
IN1-In5 to GND -0.3V to +6V
IN6P-IN7P to GND +0.3V to +6V
INM to GND -0.3V to 0.3V
RESET to GND -0.3V to +6V
SDA, SCL to GND -0.3V to +6V
ADDR to GND -0.3V to VDD + 0.3V
Continuous Power Dissipation
(TA = +70°C, derate 20.8mW/°C above +70°C)		 1666.7mW
Operating Temperature -40°C to +125°C
Junction Temperature +150°C
Storage Temperature Range -65°C to +150°C
Lead Temperture Range +300°C
