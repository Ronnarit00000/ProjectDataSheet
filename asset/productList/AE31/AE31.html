MAX25301A

Pin Description

PIN NAME FUNCTION
1,2 IN Regulator Supply Input. Connect to a voltage between 1.7V and 5.5V and bypass with a 10µF capacitor from IN to GND.
3 GND Regulator Ground. Connect IN and OUT bypass capacitor GND connections to this pin for best performance.
4 EN Enable Input. Connect this pin to a logic signal to enable (VENhigh) or disable (VENlow) the regulator output. Connect to IN to keep the output enabled whenever a valid supply voltage is present.
5 SELA Select Input A. Connect to GND, IN, or leave unconnected to select the output voltage. This pin is read only at startup.
6 SELB Select Input B. Connect to GND, IN, or leave unconnected to select the output voltage. This pin is read only at startup.
7 OUTS Output Voltage Sense Input. Connect to the load at a point where accurate regulation is required to eliminate resistive metal drops.
8 BYP Bypass Capacitor Input. Connect a capacitor with a value from 0.001µF to 0.1µF between OUT and BYP to reduce output noise and set the regulator soft-start rate.
9,10 OUT Regulator Output. Sources up to 1A at the output regulation voltage. Bypass with a 10µF (4µF minimum including voltage derating) low-ESR ( < 0.03Ω ) capacitor to GND.
EP EP Exposed Pad (TDFN only). Connect the exposed pad to a ground plane with low thermal resistance to ambient to provide the best heat sinking.

Absolute Maximum Ratings

IN, EN, POK, RSEL, BYP to GND -0.3V to +6V
FB, OUTS, SELA, SELB to GND -0.3V to +6V
OUT to GND -0.3V to IN + 0.3V
Output Short-Circuit Duration Continuous
Continuous Power Dissipation (TA = +70°C)
TDFN (derate 24.4mW/°C above 70°C)		 1951mW
Operating Temperature Range -40°C to +125°C
Maximum Junction Temperature +150°C
Storage Temperature Range -65°C to +150°C
Lead Temperature (Soldering, 10 seconds) +300°C
Soldering Temperature (reflow) +260°C

MAX25301B

Pin Description

PIN NAME FUNCTION
1,2 IN Regulator Supply Input. Connect to a voltage between 1.7V and 5.5V and bypass with a 10µF capacitor from IN to GND.
3 GND Regulator Ground. Connect IN and OUT bypass capacitor GND connections to this pin for best performance.
4 EN Enable Input. Connect this pin to a logic signal to enable (VENhigh) or disable (VENlow) the regulator output. Connect to IN to keep the output enabled whenever a valid supply voltage is present.
5 GS Ground Sense. Connect GS to GND.
6 POK Active-High Power-OK Output. Connect a pullup resistor from this pin to a supply to create a reset signal that goes high after the regulator output has reached its regulation voltage.
7 FB Feedback Divider Input. Connect a resistor-divider string from OUT to GND with the midpoint tied to this pin to set the output voltage. InTypical Application Circuits, VOUT= 0.6V x (1 + R1/R2).
8 BYP Bypass Capacitor Input. Connect a capacitor with a value from 0.001µF to 0.1µF between OUT and BYP to reduce output noise and set the regulator soft-start rate.
9,10 OUT Regulator Output. Sources up to 1A at the output regulation voltage. Bypass with a 10µF (4µF minimum including voltage derating) low-ESR ( < 0.03Ω ) capacitor to GND.
EP EP Exposed Pad (TDFN only). Connect the exposed pad to a ground plane with low thermal resistance to ambient to provide the best heat sinking.
